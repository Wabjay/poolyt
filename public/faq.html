Ridesharing occurs when there are more than one occupants in a private vehicle. Rather than travelling alone or with empty seats, drivers can connect with people travelling to same direction and share the travel cost on poolyt. These trips are made possible without the intention of making profit.

poolyt is built around safety and trust. While your location and destination is public, your contact information is only shared with members you have agreed to share a ride with.

You however need to take responsibility for your personal safety while travelling. Below are some tips to keep you safe on the road:

  • Avoid exchanging home address with members you have not met in-person
  • Arrange to join a ride from a public location

  • Never feel obligated to join a trip, especially when you have doubt about your travel rideshare partners.

On poolyt, the cost of ridesharing is called a contribution, and is a calculation based on amortized vehicle cost, amortized maintenance cost, fuel consumed per distance covered, any tolls paid on route, and a management fee to keep the service running. This is to ensure passenger cost offsets the real cost of travel with no profit making intention.

Contributions are fixed along the route and split equally for everyone riding with you. Though tipping is encouraged, never should you feel compelled to tip anyone you share a ride with.
